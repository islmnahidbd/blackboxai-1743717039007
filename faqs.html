General Questions

What documents do I need for a visa application?

The required documents vary depending on the visa type and destination country, but typically include:

  • Valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Completed application form
  • Proof of travel arrangements (flight itinerary)
  • Proof of accommodation
  • Financial statements showing sufficient funds
  • Travel insurance (for some countries)

How long does the visa processing take?

Processing times vary by visa type and destination country:

  • Tourist visas: 3-10 business days
  • Business visas: 5-15 business days
  • Student visas: 2-4 weeks
  • Work visas: 3-6 weeks

We recommend applying at least 4-6 weeks before your intended travel date to account for any delays.

Can I track my visa application status?

Yes, once you submit your application through VisaService, you'll receive:

  • A unique application reference number
  • Regular email updates at each processing stage
  • Access to our online tracking portal

Our customer support team is also available to provide status updates via phone or email.

Visa Specific Questions

What's the difference between single and multiple entry visas?

The main differences are:

  • Single Entry Visa: Allows one entry to the destination country. If you leave, you'll need a new visa to re-enter.
  • Multiple Entry Visa: Allows unlimited entries during the visa validity period. Ideal for frequent travelers.

Multiple entry visas typically cost more but provide greater flexibility. The choice depends on your travel plans.

Can I extend my visa while abroad?

Visa extension possibilities depend on:

  • The destination country's immigration policies
  • Your visa type and current status
  • The reason for your extension request

Some countries allow extensions for tourism or business, while others require you to return home and apply for a new visa. Contact us at least 2 weeks before your visa expires for assistance.
