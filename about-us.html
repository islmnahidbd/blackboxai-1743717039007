About LeogoVisa

A trusted name in visa processing with over a decade of excellence

Our Mission

At LeogoVisa, we simplify international travel by providing efficient, transparent, and personalized visa processing services. Our goal is to remove the stress from visa applications so you can focus on your journey ahead.

As a concern of Leogo Travels Ltd, we combine travel expertise with specialized visa knowledge to offer comprehensive solutions.

Our Journey

2010 - Foundation

Established as a specialized visa service under Leogo Travels Ltd, starting with student visa processing for Australia and Canada.

2014 - Expansion

Expanded services to include work visas and document authentication, opening our dedicated processing center in Gulshan.

2018 - Recognition

Awarded 'Best Visa Service Provider' by Bangladesh Tourism Board for our efficient processing and high approval rates.

2023 - Present

Now processing over 5,000 visa applications annually with a team of 15 dedicated specialists across multiple countries.
